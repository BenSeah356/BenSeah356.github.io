  1. St. Louis Gospel Center

    1918–1957
    First meetings were held in a large rented tent in University City. The church used several locations over the years, eventually moving to property at 4000 Washington Blvd. where the ministry stayed for 26 years.

  2. IFCA Founded

    1930
    Oscar B. Bottorff, founder of the St. Louis Gospel Center, was instrumental in the organization of the new movement.

  3. The First Move

    1952
    Moved to property on Lindell Blvd. Eventual name change to Lindell Blvd Bible Church in 1957.

  4. The Second Move

    1970â€”Present
    Occupied new facilities at 4111 Von Talge Rd in South St. Louis County and changed the name to South County Bible Church.

Summit Club

September–May

Youth Ministry

Summit Club, a mid-week Bible club, is an exciting new curriculum, from Discipleland, where students gather in a large group setting to experience God's Word in fun and unique ways. They then gather into small groups by age, where they learn to love God and each other. A fun game or craft time rounds out the evening. Service projects are also scheduled for the school year. Your kids will LOVE being a part of Discipleland Summit!

    Our 3-fold purpose:
  • To lead kids to know Jesus Christ
  • To teach them to live for Him
  • To prepare them to defend their faith
  • What: A high energy, fast paced, fun Bible club
  • Who: Kids 3 years old—6th grade
  • When: Wednesdays 6:45-8:15 PM | September–May
  • How: Bible lessons, workbooks, games, special events, service projects

Camp Su'Na'Go

Youth and Teen Ministry

Camp Su'Na'Go takes place at Pinecrest Campground in beautiful Ozark country near Fredericktown, Missouri. Pinecrest is a heavily wooded retreat center about 80 miles south of St Louis. It provides excellent facilities run by the Church of the Nazarene. They go out of their way to make our time there successful and rewarding. We are the only group on campus for the week.

Camp Su'Na'Go has all the sun and fun that you have come to expect from a resident summer camp. The Su'Na'Go difference is found in our top quality, nationally recognized bible teachers, challenging missionary guests, and bible memorization program.

Our well rounded program will leave your child challenged from Godâ€™s Word, excited about world-wide missions, encouraged in their Christian walk, and ready to face their world unashamed of the Gospel.

Camp Su'Na'Go is a ministry of South County Bible Church in cooperation with the Greater St. Louis IFCA Regional.

  • When: June 2018
  • Where: Pinecrest Campground, Fredericktown, MO

R.I.S.E.

(Righteously Influencing Society for Eternity)

Youth and Teen Ministry

Encouraging and Equipping Youth for Service in the Local Church

Like no other time in history, youth are looking for real answers to life's questions. They want to know truth, not just trends and traditions. Our Youth Group at SCBC, is all about communicating truth...Biblical Truth!

Our Youth Group believes in and promotes absolutes for young people like:

Bible Study

Scripture tells us to be ready always to give an answer for the reason of our hope. Young people need to know the Bible, not just about it. We provide a complete Biblical curriculum including survey, topical study and an in-depth look at doctrine.

Small Groups

Each time we gather, there is time set aside to break into smaller groups and discuss the truths discovered in the Bible Study time as well as an opportunity to share prayer requests and offer encouragement in the students walk with Christ.

Service in the Local Church

Christ did not come to be served, but to serve. In addition to a consistent intake of the Word of God, we strive to provide training opportunities for service in the church and outlets to serve in the community and neighborhood.

Scripture Memory

"These words which I command thee this day shall be in thine heart." One of the greatest spiritual weapons available to youth today is scripture memory. We participate in IFCA Bible Quizzing as an outlet for encouraging systematic memory of large portions of Godâ€™s Word.

Activities

We recognize that teens also need an outlet to just have fun and be with like-minded believers. We provide monthly activities and outings designed to encourage the inviting of new individuals (saved or unsaved) and to build lasting friendships.

IFCA Bible Quizzing

Youth and Teen Ministry

Bible Quizzing is an exciting teaching mechanism to help youth memorize Scripture through friendly competition. The National Tournament is run by IFCA. It has Senior and Novice Division Championships.

Our goal is to equip young believers with God's Word for application in their daily lives. Participants practice on Monday nights. Memorization ranges from individual verses to entire chapters.
